Cookbook of a Diabetic!!!

Add to that mix the constraint that I prefer vegetarian options often and I was soon experimenting with a lot of my pre low carb favourites to create something pallatable. As a result I have a growing collection of recipes which I hope other people following low carb may find helpful too.
